## 2. Lesson Notes / 課堂筆記
When solving equations, remember the **Golden Rule of Algebra**:
在解方程時，請記住**代數的黃金法則**：
> Whatever you do to one side of the equation, you must do exactly the same to the other side!
> 無論你對方程式的一邊做什麼，你也必須對另一邊做完全相同的事情！

If you add 5 to the left, you must add 5 to the right. The goal is always to **isolate $x$** by itself.
如果你在左邊加 5，你必須在右邊也加 5。目標始終是將 $x$ **獨立**出來。
